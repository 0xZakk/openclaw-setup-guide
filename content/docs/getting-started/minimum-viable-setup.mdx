---
title: Minimum Viable Setup
description: A phased rollout to get the most value in the least time
---

Don't try to set up everything at once. Layer things on over a couple of weeks.

## Phase 1 -- Identity and Memory (Day 1)

The foundation. Without these, your agent has no continuity between sessions.

- [Agent Identity](/docs/foundation/agent-identity) -- name, personality, operating instructions
- [Memory System](/docs/foundation/memory-system) -- daily logs + long-term curated memory
- [User Profile](/docs/foundation/user-profile) -- who you are, your goals, your preferences

**Time:** ~30 minutes of prompting and iteration.

## Phase 2 -- Life OS (Day 2)

Start getting daily value from your agent.

- [PARA Organization](/docs/foundation/para-organization) -- structure your workspace
- [Morning Check-in](/docs/life-os/morning-checkin) -- daily planning template
- [Evening Check-in](/docs/life-os/evening-checkin) -- daily reflection template
- [Heartbeat System](/docs/automation/heartbeat-system) -- proactive periodic check-ins

**Time:** ~20 minutes to set up the cron jobs.

## Phase 3 -- Knowledge Management (Week 1)

Turn your agent into a second brain.

- [LogSeq/Obsidian Vault](/docs/knowledge-management/logseq-vault) -- personal knowledge base
- [Zettelkasten Skill](/docs/knowledge-management/zettelkasten-skill) -- process any source into atomic notes
- [QMD Search](/docs/knowledge-management/qmd-search) -- semantic search across all your notes

**Time:** ~1 hour for setup, then ongoing as you feed it sources.

## Phase 4 -- Content and Development (Week 2+)

Power-user features.

- [Voice Profile](/docs/foundation/voice-profile) -- capture your writing voice for ghostwriting
- [Tweet Pipeline](/docs/content-creation/tweet-pipeline) -- turn vault notes into tweets
- [Coding Agent](/docs/development/coding-agent) -- delegate coding to sub-agents
- Infrastructure: [Brave Search](/docs/infrastructure/brave-search), [Audio Transcription](/docs/infrastructure/audio-transcription), [1Password](/docs/infrastructure/onepassword)

**Time:** Varies. Each is independent -- pick what you need.
